Manuscript Encoding and Digital Editions based on MEI

Dozent: Johannes Kepper

Zeit: Donnerstag, 27. 9., 14:00 bis 17:30 Uhr und Freitag, 28. 9., 09:00 bis 17:30 Uhr
Raum: F2.211

This course will introduce some advanced features of MEI. Participants will learn about facilities to capture manuscript details in music documents and the encoding of digital editions based on multiple sources in MEI. A discussion about effective workflows in this area is included. Participants are invited to submit their own examples in advance.

This course will be held in english, with german assistance for non-native speakers. Course materials will be provided in german and english.

Prerequesites:
Fundamental knowledge about MEI, as imparted by the introductory workshop on MEI earlier that week or the MEI Tutorial at http://music-encoding.org, is expected. Knowledge about manuscript encoding in TEI is helpful, but not required.

Software Requirements: Schema-aware XML-Editor (current Oxygen preferred), current Firefox
